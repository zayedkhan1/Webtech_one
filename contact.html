Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a blood donor? +

You can become a blood donor by filling out our donor registration form on the "Become a Donor" page. Once registered, we'll contact you with information about donation centers and upcoming blood drives.

What are the eligibility requirements for blood donation? +

Generally, donors must be at least 17 years old, weigh at least 110 pounds, and be in good health. Specific eligibility criteria may vary, so we recommend contacting us for detailed information.

How often can I donate blood? +

You can donate whole blood every 56 days. Platelet donations can be made more frequently, up to 24 times a year. We'll help you determine the best donation schedule for you.

Is my personal information secure? +

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All personal information is stored securely and only used for blood donation coordination purposes. We never share your information with third parties without your consent.
